Welcome to Rachel's pet shop!


This is a DApp extended from the Pet Shop Tutorial, please see: https://www.trufflesuite.com/tutorials/pet-shop.

There are five tabs on the navigation bar: Home, Adopt, Supply, Donate, Admin.
The 'Home' tab will lead you to an introduction of this DApp.
You can adopt a pet on page 'Adopt'.
You can acquire a supply on page 'Supply'.
The 'Donate' tab will take you to the page where you can donate an item.
If you are an administrator, you can go to 'Admin' page and login.
Username: Rachel
Password: rachel
After login, you will be able to add an offline donation record to the system.
